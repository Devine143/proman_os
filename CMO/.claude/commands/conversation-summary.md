# Conversation Summary

Generate a narrative summary of our discussion that captures not just what was discussed, but how our understanding evolved throughout the conversation. Follow the `Instructions` and output in the `Report Format`.

## Instructions

- Review the entire conversation history to identify key topics, decisions, and turning points
- Identify breakthrough moments where understanding shifted or deepened
- Track how thinking evolved - note where initial assumptions changed
- Capture the causal connections between insights (how one realization led to another)
- Focus on the intellectual journey, not just information preservation
- Note both explicit conclusions and implicit shifts in approach
- Identify the current position within the evolving understanding
- Determine logical next steps that build on developed insights

## Workflow

1. Scan the full conversation for major topic areas and themes
2. Identify 2-4 key breakthrough moments and their significance
3. Map the evolution of thinking (initial position -> shifts -> current understanding)
4. Note any decisions made or approaches adopted
5. Determine where we currently stand in the exploration
6. Identify what remains to be explored and why it matters

## Report Format

Respond using this exact structure:

**SUMMARY:** [Narrative description showing evolution of both topics and understanding. Include:
- What sparked the discussion
- Key breakthrough moments and their implications
- How understanding shifted over time
- Causal connections between insights
- Decisions made and their rationale]

**CURRENT POINT:** [Where we are in our evolving understanding:
- Our current position/conclusion
- Why this point is significant
- How it reflects our learning journey
- What we now understand that we didn't before]

**NEXT:** [What we're exploring next:
- Specific next steps or questions
- What we hope to learn
- How this builds on our developing insights
- Open questions that remain]
